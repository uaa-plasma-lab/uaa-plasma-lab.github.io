# Plasma Teaching and Outreach (PTO)

The UAA Plasma Lab supports undergraduate plasma-physics education at
UAA — including a hands-on plasma diagnostics laboratory course
covering Langmuir probes, microwave interferometry, and optical
emission spectroscopy — and engages the broader Alaska community
through outreach activities.
